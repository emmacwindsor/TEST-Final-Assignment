Welcome to Blend & Snap

This is a creative outlet to promote and celebrate all the aspects of dance music and all the interesting people who make this culture so exciting. This space is to show people the intricate world of dance music and the selectors and producers who are shaping it past, present, and future. The goal is to turn you onto things you may have never known about, expose you to amazing people who Blend & Snap genuinely support, and explore some socially important topics that are at forefront of keeping this community thriving. Join us on this journey and explore below what we have already been working on. This is only the beginning!
